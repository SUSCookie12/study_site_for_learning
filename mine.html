Here are some useful Minecraft commands you can use:

/gamemode creative
Switches your gamemode to Creative.
/tp [player] [target]
Teleports a player to another player or location.
/give [player] [item] [amount]
Gives an item to a player.
/summon [entity]
Summons an entity to your location.
/time set day
Sets the time to day.
/gamerule keepInventory true
Keep your Inventory when you Die in Minecraft.
